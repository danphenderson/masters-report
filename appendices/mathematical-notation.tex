\section{Mathematical Notation}
\label{app:mathematical-notation}

\begin{notation}[Report mathematical notation]
\label{not:global-mathematical-notation}
This appendix records the shared mathematical shorthand that remains active in
this report after the main text has been narrowed to the implemented 1D solver,
verification records, and diagnostic velocity comparison. Elementary
function-space definitions and compactness background are not repeated here.
\end{notation}
