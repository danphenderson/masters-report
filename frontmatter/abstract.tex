\noindent\textbf{Abstract.}\par\smallskip
This report develops a mathematical and numerical account of idealized stenotic blood-flow
simulation. The mathematical framework fixes the continuum, constitutive, side-conditions,
model-hierarchy, and output conventions needed to move from three-dimensional (3D) incompressible Navier–Stokes and generalized-Newtonian models
to reduced 0D, 1D, 2D, and coupled hemodynamic formulations.
