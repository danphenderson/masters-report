\medskip
\noindent\textbf{Abstract.}\par\smallskip
This report develops a mathematical and numerical account of idealized stenotic blood-flow
simulation. The framework fixes the continuum, constitutive, boundary-data,
side-condition, model-hierarchy, and output conventions needed to relate
three-dimensional (3D) incompressible Navier--Stokes,
fluid--structure interaction, and generalized-Newtonian models to reduced 0D,
1D, 2D, and coupled hemodynamic formulations.
