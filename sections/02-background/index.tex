\section{Focused Background and Literature Review}
\label{sec:background-literature-review}

This chapter reviews only the literature needed to frame the three research
questions: stenosis-aware 1D area--flow models, closure dependence,
equilibrium-preserving balance-law methods, implementation verification,
cross-dimensional observation operators, and the distinction between verification,
diagnostic cross-model comparison, and external validation. It is not a
comprehensive systematic review, because no search protocol is supplied.
